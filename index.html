What's the best build for pushing Pit 150+ as a new Spiritborn?
S
For Pit 150+ as Spiritborn, Quill Volley is your best bet. It's currently the highest-performing build in the game, and relatively accessible to gear.

Why Quill Volley?
  • Damage: Quill Volley scales exceptionally with the Eagle spirit, dealing ~2.4T DPS with mid-tier gear
  • Survivability: Built-in dodge from Eagle spirit + Soar for repositioning
  • Clear speed: Excellent AoE for both trash and elites

Key gear priorities:
  • Moonrise (unique amulet) — essential for Eagle stack uptime
  • Tempest Roar (helm) — if you don't have Harlequin Crest
  • Temper for Quill Volley ranks and Crit Damage

Yes, and what's the minimum gear I need before attempting Pit 150?
S
Minimum gear checklist for Pit 150:
  • Weapon: Any GA polearm with 3/4 Greater Affixes — prioritize Crit Damage and Vulnerable Damage
  • Moonrise amulet: Non-negotiable for this build
  • Armor: At least 18,000+ armor (aim for 20K+)
  • Tempering: Quill Volley ranks on both weapons, Crit Damage on jewelry
  • Paragon: Complete Eagle + Centipede boards with all rare glyphs at level 15+
  • Resistances: All at 70%+ after Paragon

With this setup, Pit 150 should be comfortable. For 160+, you'll want to start farming for Mythic uniques and leveling glyphs to 21.
